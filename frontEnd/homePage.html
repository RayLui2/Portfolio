Poly Properties - Online Leasing Platform

Poly Properties is a website application developed to help college students at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo make decisions and gain more knowledge about different housing listings in San Luis Obispo. It provides ratings and reviews for local housing listings, enabling students to evaluate properties before signing a lease and deciding to live at a property. Students will be able to leave reviews based on personal experience and any feedback on the property itself or the landlord to help other students in the future gain more knowledge about the property they are looking to rent/live at. This can also be used to landlords’ benefit and see how they can improve their property.

Digital MultiMeter

The STM32L476 NUCLEO-L467RG Development Board was programmed to function as a digital multimeter in this project. The digital multimeter takes an input signal, AC or DC, as an input, and can measure frequency, peak-to-peak voltage, and true root mean square voltage for AC signals, and can measure DC voltage for DC signals. For both AC and DC signals, the digital multimeter is able to measure voltages from 0 - 3.3 V. As for the AC signals as inputs, the digital multimeter is able to read and measure sine, triangle, sawtooth, and square waves. The measured values for AC and DC signals are displayed neatly in the terminal through USART, with bar graphs for each voltage measurement. The digital multimeter is accurate within 10 mV for voltage measurements, and 1 Hz for frequency measurements
